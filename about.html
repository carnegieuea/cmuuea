UEA Membership

Become a member of the UEA to stay more involved and active in Carnegie Mellon’s entrepreneurial scene.

Benefits
  • Stay up to date on all entrepreneurial events in and around Pittsburgh
  • Meet fellow students with similar entrepreneurial aspirations
  • Network with entrepreneurial leaders in the Pittsburgh community
  • Attend UEA member exclusive events. More info coming soon!
  • Preference for UEA leadership positions

Requirements
  • Open to all Carnegie Mellon students
  • No previous entrepreneurial experience necessary
  • Attend 4 entrepreneurial events per semester (eligible events available here)

Hack-a-Startup

Hack-a-Startup is a hackathon meets business plan competition geared towards interdisciplinary teams. Build an innovative product with your friends over the weekend and compete for prizes for the best business idea and execution.

CMU Venture Challenge

CMUVC gives undergraduates the chance to win thousands of dollars for their existing startups and gain invaluable mentorship from key entrepreneurs in the Pittsburgh area and beyond. Applications opening up soon.

Elevator Pitch

Have an idea and want some funding to get it started? Compete in the Elevator Pitch competition during Global Entrepreneurship Week at CMU. Pitch your idea to a panel of judges like Shark Tank and compete against the brightest entrepreneurs.
